<h1 style="text-align: center">PathWise AI</h1>

<div>
AI based learning platform with finding opportunities based on your learning status and mastery on the topics. It gives you a proper place to freely expand your knowledge and explore other fields with AI.
</div>

---

# Components
1. **AI Playground**: You can ask doubt, solve question and build your ideas.
2. **Learning**: You can look at your mastery on topics and subtopics.
3. **Quiz**: You can attempt quizzes based on your learning.
4. **Roadmap**: You can build your roadmap to make your path smoother.

---

# Ideas
* You will get direct response from AI for your doubts, questions.
* AI will give you systematic roadmap if you want to build your roadmap then tell and AI will build your customized roadmap.
* AI will generate MCQs to test your knowledge.
